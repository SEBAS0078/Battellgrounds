Battellgrounds

Battellgrounds is New Battell’s first building-wide competition. Floors earn points through monthly events, participation, and keeping common spaces clean. At the end of the year, the winning floor takes home the New Battell Cup and an extra budget for a celebration.

How are points calculated?

Points for monthly events are divided into two categories: Participation and Competition Results.

Participation

Each floor earns a participation score based on the percentage of its residents who attend the event. The higher the percentage, the better your floor’s participation score.

Competition Results

Additional points are awarded based on floor placements in event competitions:

  • 1st Place: 200 points
  • 2nd Place: 100 points
  • 3rd Place: 60 points
  • 4th Place: 40 points

If multiple floors tie, they share the placement points equally.
